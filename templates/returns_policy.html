Returns & Exchange Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

7-Day Return Policy

At Dhami Electronics, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a 7-day return policy on most products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 7 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Note: The 7-day return period starts from the date of delivery.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Unused Condition: Product must be unused, unwashed, and in the same condition as received
  • Original Packaging: All original packaging, tags, accessories, and manuals must be included
  • Proof of Purchase: Original invoice or order confirmation is required
  • Non-Damaged: Product should not show signs of damage caused by the customer

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Software, games, or digital products that have been opened or used
  • Personal care items, headphones, and earbuds (for hygiene reasons)
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Items damaged due to misuse, accident, or improper handling
  • Products missing serial numbers or warranty stickers
  • Customized or personalized products

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will:

  • Arrange for a replacement at no additional cost
  • Cover all return shipping costs for defective items
  • Process a full refund if replacement is not available

Important: Please inspect your package immediately upon delivery and report any damage within 48 hours.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued through the original payment method:

  • Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer or store credit
  • Bank Transfer: Refund to the same bank account
  • Mobile Payment: Refund to the same mobile wallet
  • Store Credit: Available upon request

Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after inspection.

Return Shipping

Customer's Responsibility: For non-defective returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Our Responsibility: For defective or incorrect items, we will provide a prepaid shipping label or arrange for pickup.

Return Address: Dhami Electronics, Dhangadhi, Nepal

Exchange Process

If you wish to exchange an item for a different product or size:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery
  2. Return the original item (following return guidelines)
  3. Place a new order for the desired item
  4. We'll process a refund for the returned item upon inspection

Warranty Information

All products come with manufacturer warranty as specified on the product page. Warranty periods vary by product and brand:

  • Electronics: 6 months to 1 year warranty
  • Accessories: 3 to 6 months warranty
  • Premium Products: Up to 2 years warranty

For warranty claims, please contact us with your order number and a description of the issue. We'll assist you with the manufacturer's warranty process.

How to Initiate a Return

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at returns@dhamielectronics.com with your order number
  2. Include photos/videos of the product (if reporting damage or defect)
  3. Wait for return authorization and instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in original packaging
  5. Ship to our return address or wait for pickup
